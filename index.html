Confirmed! Areece’s Paradise 2 On Its Final Stages. Rapper A-Reece has been keeping himself off the spotlight because the plan is to give fans a ground breaking project.

The 23 year-old announced that he will be releasing Paradise 2 when the year began, after a number of teases and speculations surrounding the project, some fans lost hope that it might drop anytime soon. The good news is that it is on its final stages, almost ready to reach fans.

To confirm these great news was A-Reece’s girlfriend Rickell Jones on Instagram stories. She expressed that she has been getting a lot of questions on the release of Paradise 2 and decided to finally give fans something to hold on to as hope to receive the Selfish rapper’s project sooner.

“Okay so since I see this question coming up a lot I am going to clear the air, the album is on the way. As far as I know they are mixing and mastering,” wrote Jones.
